Professional Medical Advice

General Health Tips

1. Stay hydrated by drinking at least 8 glasses of water daily.

2. Get 7-8 hours of quality sleep each night.

3. Wash your hands frequently to prevent infections.

4. Don't skip meals and maintain a balanced diet.

Medication Guidelines

1. Always take medications as prescribed by your doctor.

2. Never share prescription medications with others.

3. Store medicines in a cool, dry place away from sunlight.

4. Check expiration dates before taking any medication.

When to See a Doctor

1. Persistent fever lasting more than 3 days.

2. Difficulty breathing or chest pain.

3. Sudden severe headache or vision changes.

4. Unexplained weight loss or gain.
